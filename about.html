ACKLI HOLIDAYS (PVT) LTD

Ackli Holidays is a diverse group of creative, self-motivated individuals with a passion for giving back to the people and places we visit and empower our travelers to do the same.

Ackli Holidays is your one-stop premier partner for an enchanting journey through the captivating island of Sri Lanka. With our unrivaled expertise and passion for travel, we invite you to embark on an unforgettable adventure that will awaken your senses and leave you with cherished memories that last a lifetime. At Ackli Holidays, we believe that travel is not just about visiting new places; it's about immersing yourself in the rich tapestry of culture, heritage, and natural beauty that Sri Lanka has to offer.

Our dedicated team of travel experts is committed to curating tailor-made experiences that cater to your individual preferences, ensuring every aspect of your trip is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed. Ackli Holidays boasts customer-centric services that focus on the needs and comforts of our clients. Our team of experts is dedicated to putting our customers' needs far above their own. Let Ackli Holidays be your passport to the wonders of Sri Lanka, where every moment is an extraordinary experience waiting to be unveiled.

Ackli Holidays, with over a decade of experience, specializes in personalized journeys across Sri Lanka. From stunning beaches to ancient sites, we showcase the island's beauty, culture, and hospitality. Explore with us!
